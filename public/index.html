// FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before Revival

Event Details

What is the program schedule? +

Open house begins at 8:00 AM, and the first session starts at 9:00 AM. Lunch break will be from 12:00 PM to 1:30 PM, followed by the afternoon session.

What time does the event start and end? +

The retreat will run from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Participants are encouraged to arrive early during the open house, which begins at 8:00 AM.

Where is the venue? +

The retreat will be held at Victory Bulacan Complex.

Is parking available? +

Yes, parking will be available at the venue.

Registration

Is there a registration fee? +

This retreat has already been invested in by people who believe in the next generation. Just bring yourself, an open heart, and a willingness to encounter God.

Can I walk in on the day of the event? +

To help us prepare well for everyone, only registered participants will be accommodated. Registration is open until May 10, 2026, at 11:59 PM.

Can I invite my friends? +

Yes. You may invite friends who are part of our Leaders and Serving Team Members.

Can I attend Day 1 only? +

The retreat is designed as a complete two-day experience, so we encourage and expect all participants to attend the full retreat.

Preparation

What should I wear? +

Wear something comfortable and appropriate for the retreat. We also encourage you to bring a jacket in case the venue gets cold.

What should I bring? +

Please bring your Bible, notebook and pen, jacket, tumbler or water bottle, and an expectant heart.

During the Retreat

Where can I get lunch? +

You may bring your own food or meals to share with friends. During the lunch break, you may also order food or eat outside at your own pace. Suggested food options and an order assistance station will also be available to help you arrange your meals.

Are phones allowed? +

Yes, phones are allowed. We simply encourage everyone to use them responsibly, minimize distractions, and stay fully present during the sessions.

Who can I approach if I have questions? +

You may approach our campus missionaries for any concerns or assistance. They will be glad to help you.
